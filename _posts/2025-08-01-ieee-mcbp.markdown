**Multimodal McBP-Net**: hybrid CNN-LSTM for continuous beat-to-beat BP estimation from PPG, ECG, IPG, and skin temperature. Achieves MAE of **4.19 and 2.98 mmHg** for SBP/DBP. Published in **IEEE JBHI**.
